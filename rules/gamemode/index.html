Gamemode Rules

The things you should follow when playing on specific gamemode (Last updated: Jan 12, 2021)

Co-op Gamemode Rules (L4D2, SC, OC)

  1. Map Rushing is not permitted. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  2. Intentional friendly fire on specific games are not allowed. (If there are 2 or more players in-game, bots are not counted)
  3. Intentional body-blocking is not permitted.
  4. Trolling on some maps (e.g. Destroying the Ally structures which results in team loss, killing the npc which requires to pass from specific area, etc) are not allowed.
    5. Note Sometimes people makes mistake, non-intentional friendly fire will not get restriction.

Zombie Escape Gamemode Rules

  1. Intentionally stalling the game is not permitted.
  2. Do not truce with zombie team.
  3. Map rushing is not permitted.
  4. Intentional trolling(Such as blocking the way with props) is not allowed.

Machine Attacks

  1. Map Rushing is not permitted. (If there are 2 or more players in-game)
  2. Do not intentionally kill teammates with elevator.

VS. Saxton Hale/Freak Fortress (TF2)

  1. Do not suicide as Boss/Death/Hale.
  2. Do not stall the round such as camping.
  3. Do not block the Boss/Death/Hale with Engineer teleporters.
  4. Do not farm rage as Boss/Death/Hale such as farming rage such as taking damage.
    5. Note Engineers are allowed to camp, but only with an active sentry placed down.

Notes

  1. The following rules can be changed at any time with notice.
  2. There is 7 days grace period for gamemode rule changes.
  3. You may receive restriction for obstructing the use of the community or doing a clear and malicious act.
