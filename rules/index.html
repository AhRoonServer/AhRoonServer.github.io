Community Rules

The things you should follow when using our services (Last updated: Dec 9, 2020)

Community Rules

This applies to all Mystic Moonlight services.

  1. Be nice to other members! Any harassment, rudeness, toxic behaviors are unacceptable.
  2. Do not spam on chat or voice chat.
  3. Advertising other website or community is forbidden, which is not related to Mystic Moonlight projects.
  4. Respect your/other's privacy. Never give your/other's personal information to anyone.
  5. NSFW/Offensive contents are forbidden. This includes anything you upload to community.
  6. Do not abuse our support system such as report, CallAdmin, ModMail and so on.
  7. Please refrain from making non-existent rules.
  8. Any scam attempts or unapproved transactions are not allowed. This includes trying to scam other's account, selling account that does not belong to you, etc.
    9. Note Playing music through voice chat is allowed except disturbing music.

In-Game Rules

  1. You are not allowed to abuse vote system. This includes votekicking the person without any valid reason.
  2. Some gamemode might have own specific rules. Gamemode Rules can be read here.
  3. You may not abuse in-game bug, glitch or exploits. Instead report them to any staff member.
  4. Do not use unauthorized third-party clients or mods which gains unfair advantage than other players.
    5. Note Gamemode Rules has more priority than other rules.

Discord Rules

  1. Follow the discord's ToS and Community Guidelines.
  2. You need to be at least 13 years old to join our discord server, according to Discord ToS.
  3. Do not @mention other person without reason.
  4. Post your message on right channel! Read the pinned post, channel info for more information about the current channel.
    5. Note Any message which breaks Discord ToS will be removed immediately.

Warnings

  1. You will receive warning point when breaking point, and your restriction will be increased if you keep receiving warning point for same reason.
  2. Each warning points will be expired after 30 days.
  3. You may appeal your false warnings on our Discord, using the ModMail. Make sure you include more details about your false warnings and provide evidence if needed.
    4. Note Please note that while your restriction is active, the expiration of warning point will not be counted.
    Note Only false warning point can be appealed.

Notes

  1. The following rules can be changed at any time with notice.
  2. There is 7 days grace period for rules changes.
