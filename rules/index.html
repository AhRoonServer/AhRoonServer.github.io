Community Rules

Things to follow when you use our services (Last updated on March 4, 2021)
Community Rules

This applies to all Mystic Moonlight services.

  1. Be nice and respectful to other users! Any toxic behaviors such as harassment, rudeness, racism are not tolerated.
  2. Do not spam.[1]
  3. Advertising other website or community which is not related to Ah_Roon and Mystic Moonlight are prohibited.
  4. Respect your/other's privacy. Never give your/other's personal information to anyone.
  5. NSFW or offensive contents are forbidden. This includes anything you upload to community.[2]
  6. Do not abuse our support system such as Tickets, CallAdmin, ModMail and so on.
  7. Please refrain from making non-existent rules.
  8. Any scam attempts or unapproved transactions are not allowed. This includes trying to scam other's account, selling account that does not belong to you, etc.
  9. Using alternative account to bypass bans are strictly not allowed. All associated account will be permanently banned.
  10. Talking about piracy content that is not genuiue products are forbidden. We do not support piracy content and never will.
  11. Stop excessively being nitpicky. If you have criticism, please tell them politely.
In-Game Rules
  1. You are not allowed to abuse vote system. This includes votekicking the person without any valid reason.
  2. Some gamemodes might have own specific rules. Gamemode Rules can be read here.
  3. Gamemode Rules has more priority than other rules.
  4. You may not abuse in-game bug, glitch or exploits. Instead report them to any staff member.
  5. Do not use unauthorized third-party clients or mods which gains unfair advantage than other players.
Discord Rules
  1. Follow the Discord Terms of Services and Community Guidelines. Any message which breaks Discord ToS will be removed immediately without warning.
  2. You need to be at least 13 years old to join our discord server as mentioned in the Terms of Services.
  3. Do not @mention other person without valid reason.
  4. Post your message on right channel! Read the pinned post, channel info for more information about the current channel.
  5. The serious topics such as drama, and real life incidents can only be posted on #serious-chat to keep chat atmosphere positive.
Restrictions
  1. You will receive warning if you break the rules for first time, if you already have previous warning for same reason in the past, you will receive restriction such as ban, mute, gag. (e.g. Trolling = Ban, Chat or Voice offense = Gag or mute)
  2. Permanent mute, ban, gag can be given if offense is severe at moderator's discretion. (Such as cheating in-game, ban evasion, scam attempts, etc.)
  3. You may appeal your false restriction on our Discord by creating ticket, or using the ModMail. Make sure you include more details about your false warnings and provide evidence if needed.[3]
Note
  1. The following rules can be changed at any time with notice.
  2. You may receive restriction for obstructing the use of the community or doing a clear and malicious act.
